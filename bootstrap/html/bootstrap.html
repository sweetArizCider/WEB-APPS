This is Beabadoobee

Beabadoobee is a Filipino-born English singer-songwriter and musician. Her real name is Beatrice Kristi Ilejay Laus, and she was born on June 3, 2000, in Iloilo City, Philippines. She later moved to London, England, where she grew up and developed her passion for music.

More about

Beabadoobee (born Beatrice Laus) is a Filipino-British singer-songwriter known for her indie rock and bedroom pop music. She gained popularity with her song "Coffee" which went viral on TikTok. Beabadoobee's music often features a mix of acoustic and electric sounds, drawing inspiration from 90s alternative rock.

Beabadoobee has achieved significant milestones in her career, including:

  • Nominated for the Rising Star Award at the Brit Awards 2020.
  • Her debut album "Fake It Flowers" received critical acclaim and charted in several countries.
  • She has amassed a large following on social media and streaming platforms, with millions of followers and monthly listeners.
