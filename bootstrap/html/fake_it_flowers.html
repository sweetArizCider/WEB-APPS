Produced by Joseph Rodgers & Pete Robertson Written by Joseph Rodgers, Pete Robertson “Sorry” is a song about Bea’s childhood friend. During their years together, they lived a wild, experimental life which included messing with drugs. Such a habit overtook Bea’s friend, so she separated herself from this individual. And even in the present day, the friend is still dealing with this issue. Bea is not only “sorry” for having to excommunicate this person, even though for the most part she still feels a need to do so. But she is also feeling partially to blame, so to speak, for this individual falling into such a trap in the first place.
